# Photo Organization Guide for John Chezik's Professional Gallery

## 📁 Folder Structure

When you upload your photos, please organize them in the following structure:

```
public/
├── photos/
│   ├── [photo-name].jpg          # Full resolution photos (1920x1080 or higher)
│   └── thumbnails/
│       └── [photo-name].jpg      # Thumbnail versions (400x300 or similar)
```

## 📐 Image Specifications

### Full Resolution Photos:
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Dimensions**: Minimum 1920x1080 (16:9) or 1080x1080 (1:1)
- **File Size**: Under 2MB for optimal loading
- **Quality**: High quality, professional photography

### Thumbnail Photos:
- **Format**: JPG
- **Dimensions**: 400x300 (4:3 aspect ratio)
- **File Size**: Under 100KB
- **Quality**: Optimized for fast loading

## 🔧 Next Steps

1. **Upload your photos** to the `public/photos/` folder
2. **Create thumbnails** and place them in `public/photos/thumbnails/`
3. **Name them professionally** using the conventions above
4. **I'll update the gallery data** with your actual photos and professional descriptions

## 📝 Photo Information Needed

For each photo, I'll need:
- **Title**: Professional, descriptive title
- **Description**: 1-2 sentences about the photo
- **Category**: studio, live, behind-scenes, personal, events, merchandise
- **Date**: When the photo was taken
- **Location**: Where it was taken
- **Photographer**: Who took the photo
- **Tags**: Relevant keywords for search
